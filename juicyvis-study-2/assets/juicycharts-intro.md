# PART II: Play the Infographics!

This is the second, as well as the main body, of this study. In this part, you will be randomly given a total of 7 interactable Infographics/diagrams/charts, **some of which may have, and some may not have** embellishments/juicy feedback (such as animation, visual arts, and sound effects). For each of the infographics, you will be asked to interact with them, gain the answer for tasks, and report your experience of using or playing them.

Whenever you finish your answer, you will be able to click the **[Next]** button, to move to the next page of the study. We suggest maximizing your browser window, as the infographics/diagrams/charts can be on the larger side.
