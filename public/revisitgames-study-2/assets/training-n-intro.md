# Training Session: NOT-Having-Navigation-System Group

You are assigned to the group that plays the game without navigation systems. 

For the no navigation system setting:

- You don't have mini-map or other types of hint, but you still have access to a full-screen pop-up map (invoked with the **M** key) that only shows the target destination.

<img src="./assets/game_N_01.png" width="auto" height="300px">

---

Whenever you finish a game map (no matter if you successfully navigated to the destinations, or not), you will see a tip of **"This is the end of the game. Please return to the survey."** on your screen as shown in below:

<img src="./assets/complete.png" width="auto" height="600px">

Please click the [Next] button to move to the next page of the study **ONLY when you see the complete tip. Otherwise, it may result in a failure to record your data and ultimately lead to a failure of your study.**
