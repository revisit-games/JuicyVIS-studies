# Training Session: Having-Strong-Navigation-System Group

You are assigned to the group that plays the game with strong navigation systems. 

For the strong navigation system setting:

- You can have an always-on mini-map in the top right corner of the screen, and 
- An additional hint of the path to the destination with a solid yellow line permanently drawn in the ground. 
- You also have access to a full-screen pop-up map (invoked with the **M** key) that shows the target destination, your location, and the path hint.
